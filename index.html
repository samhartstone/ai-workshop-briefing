Welcome

This is your official AI Session briefing station. Before the session, please complete the actions below so you are set up and ready to participate.

Required actions

Two quick things to sort before the workshop: make sure you can access Microsoft Copilot, then check you can open the shared workshop materials.

✦

Access Microsoft Copilot

Before the workshop, please ensure you can access Microsoft Copilot on your laptop and, if useful, on your work mobile device.

Desktop setup

The easiest option is to search for Copilot using the Windows Start menu and pin it to your taskbar for quick access during the session.

Alternatively, open a browser and launch Copilot from the Microsoft 365 App Launcher — the nine-dot menu located in the top-left corner of the Microsoft 365 homepage.

Mobile setup

Copilot can also be installed on your work mobile device.

Open the Company Portal app and search for Microsoft Copilot. If you use a work-issued iPhone, Company Portal should already be installed and available on your device.

↗

Access the workshop materials

During the workshop we will be working through practical exercises using shared source material.

Please access the folder in advance and ensure you can open the contents successfully before attending the session.

Open workshop materials → Check access before the session.

You do not need to prepare polished outputs before the session. Just bring access, curiosity and a working Copilot setup.
